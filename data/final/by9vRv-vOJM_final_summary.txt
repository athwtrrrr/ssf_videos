In Australia, nearly 7.6 million tons of food is wasted per year. 80% of this waste is sent to landfill creating 3% of our total emissions. Typical household composting takes months to complete, often resulting in more food waste being produced than can be composted. Lita is an invesy automated by IoT, producing a carefree, highquality composting solution.
