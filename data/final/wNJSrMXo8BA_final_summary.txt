Coral reefs among the most biologically diverse and ecologically important ecosystems on Earth. But they are increasingly threatened by coral bleaching triggered by heat stress and ocean acidification. Coral probiotic have been shown to induce coral resistant to stress and diseases which can help mitigate bleaching event in the coral system.
