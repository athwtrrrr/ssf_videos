In Japan, scientists discovered a bacteria that is able to break down plastic. This bacteria is called Idinolais. The enzyme does so far only work on PET plastics and this is the only bacteria in the world known to eat plastic. Some of these plastics end up in the ocean and they break down until they form microplastics.
