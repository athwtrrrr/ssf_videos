Malaysia has a dependency on fossil fuels for the production of electricity. Most hydroelectric power plants in Malaysia are still utilizing coal in electricity production. For a country that is strategically located between the oceans, it will be wise for them to utilize ocean thermal energy conversion, also known as OTCH.
