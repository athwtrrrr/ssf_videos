An endoyte, simply put, is a fungal microorganism that resides in the living tissues of plant species. What they offer to the plant is protection from pathogens, which is a fancy term given to microorganisms that are capable of causing sickness and disease. They also do is help facilitate the uptake of essential nutrients from the environment.
