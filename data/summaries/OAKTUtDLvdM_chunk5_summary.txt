# VIDEO: Design for Change: Showcase 2021 | CHANNEL: Shaping STEMS Futures  | DATE: 2025-09-01

The leader system will know when organic waste has been deposited via an infrared sensor trigger and begin the monitoring process. Throughout the composting life cycle, the user will be able to monitor the decomposition process via a graphical display of the sensor information. Logs will also be recorded whenever the compost is stirred or whenever food is deposited to give the user the peace of mind that the system is operating correctly.
