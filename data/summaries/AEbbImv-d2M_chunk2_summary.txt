# VIDEO: Start Talking  Transforming the Communication Skills of Swinburne Science Students to Develop Future | CHANNEL: Shaping STEMS Futures  | DATE: 2025-10-13

Swimburn's communication for scientists program has seen over 4,800 registrations to date and over 4 and a half thousand certificates completed by approximately 2 and a. half thousand students. 455 students have achieved emerging leader status. When we embed emerging leader, we embed one certificate only with the aim that students will extend themselves to complete another three.
