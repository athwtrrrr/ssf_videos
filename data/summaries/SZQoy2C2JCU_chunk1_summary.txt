# VIDEO: Workshop 3: How to Pitch (2025) | CHANNEL: Shaping STEMS Futures  | DATE: 2025-09-24

Minshin Tan gives tips and tricks on how to pitch your startup idea to investors. He says the key to success is to be confident in your pitch and to be able to sell your idea. He also says that if you are going to pitch to an investor, you should be confident that they will back your startup.
