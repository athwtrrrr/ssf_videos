# VIDEO: Shaping STEM Futures: Climate Change in Malaysia | CHANNEL: Shaping STEMS Futures  | DATE: 2025-11-09

In Malaysia alone, 4,000 tons of edible food is thrown away each day, enough to feed 10 million people every single day. The Lost Food Project is an NGO dedicated to a sustainable future by rescuing lost food and finding a new home with people who need it most.
