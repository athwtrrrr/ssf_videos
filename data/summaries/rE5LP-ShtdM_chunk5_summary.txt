# VIDEO: Start Talking Live Showcase 2024 | CHANNEL: Shaping STEMS Futures  | DATE: 2025-09-24

Australian students from foundation to year 8 have access to almost 50% less science content than students in high performing countries such as England and America. Biological science is at the heart of understanding life itself, but in Australia, it's often overshadowed by other subjects. Imagine an approach where biology isn't just taught but experienced through dynamic, multimodal, and gamebased learning.
