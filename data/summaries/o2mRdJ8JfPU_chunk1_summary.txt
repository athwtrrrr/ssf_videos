Swinburn University of Technology is hosting a one hour session on effective storytelling with data visualization. Dr. Beta Zepharanolu, a senior lecturer at Zimban Sarawa, will talk about the importance of a good data visualization in telling the story. He will also share some of the technique that you can use while trying to tell a story using your visualizations. And lastly, he will share some tools that he personally use to create data visualization and share an example project.
