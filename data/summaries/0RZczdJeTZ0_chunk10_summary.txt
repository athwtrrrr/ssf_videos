# VIDEO: Beyond Inclusion | CHANNEL: Shaping STEMS Futures  | DATE: 2025-11-09

Too often engagement with First Nations communities is tokenistic. It's consultation after decisions have already been made. So when you're going into projects with a legacy of distrust and wasted time, you do actually have to put in the groundwork and build up that respect. Find your big three. Who are the traditional custodians of the place you feel most connected to. Mind, this is your area of expertise. Don't let your fear of saying the wrong thing deter you from ever saying anything.
