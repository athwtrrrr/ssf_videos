    A panel discussion on professional networking for STEM students, part of the "Start Talking" public speaking competition, featured professionals from recruitment, entrepreneurship, government, and career education. The panelists shared their diverse and often non-linear career journeys, emphasizing that paths are rarely straightforward. A central theme was the critical role of effective communication, with speakers advising students to tailor their language to different audiences, such as translating complex technical concepts for non-specialist teams. Key communication skills highlighted included active listening, asking insightful questions, and structuring interview answers clearly using methods like the STAR technique. The discussion also addressed emerging career trends, particularly the impact of AI, which panelists agreed will change jobs rather than eliminate them. This technological shift increases the value of uniquely human skills like creativity, critical thinking, and emotional intelligence.
