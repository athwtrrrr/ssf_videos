    The Design for Change 2021 showcase, an initiative funded by an Adobe grant, featured three finalist teams from Swinburne University presenting their STEM prototype designs. The program aimed to develop students' technical and communication skills by having them create a startup concept aligned with one of two visions: inspiring girls and women into STEM or making the world safer in partnership with Insurance Australia Group (IAG). The first finalist, Labsock, presented a mobile platform designed to make STEM less intimidating for high school students
