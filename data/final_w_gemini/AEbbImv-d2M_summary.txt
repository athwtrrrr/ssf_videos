    Swinburne University's "Start Talking" initiative addresses the challenge that science students often possess strong technical abilities but lack the soft skills, particularly communication, that are increasingly demanded by employers. The program is embedded within the core second-year "Communication for Scientists" unit, which uses authentic, work-integrated assessments to train students in communicating scientific information to diverse audiences.
