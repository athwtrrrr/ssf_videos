    The Start Talking 2021 showcase was a STEM public speaking competition for Swinburne University students, featuring six finalists, including the first participants from Sarawak, Malaysia. Each finalist presented a four-minute video on a STEM topic, followed by a live Q&A with a panel of judges from academia and industry. Matthew's presentation detailed the discovery of bacteria that can decompose PET plastic into harmless, recyclable chemicals in approximately 1.5 years, offering a significant environmental improvement. Julian proposed using machine learning to accelerate the discovery of therapeutic drugs for HPV-related cervical cancer, noting the primary challenge is limited available data. Celestina advocated for Malaysia to adopt Ocean Thermal Energy Conversion (OTEC), a renewable energy source that uses ocean temperature differences to generate electricity.
