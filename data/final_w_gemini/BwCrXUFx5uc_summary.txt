    Linda from Evolve Scientific, a recruitment agency specializing in science and technical fields, outlines the process for graduates stepping into industry. Recruitment agencies act as a free intermediary for candidates, connecting them with companies that value the speed and specialized shortlisting an agency provides. The benefits of using an agency include faster processing, expert interview preparation, access to multiple opportunities, and having a recruiter advocate for a candidate's skills and personality beyond their resume. Job seekers will encounter permanent contracts, which offer direct employment with benefits and a probation period, and casual contracts, which are through the agency and include a 25% pay loading but lack benefits and job security.

For resumes, graduates should place qualifications at the top, include all work experience to demonstrate transferable skills, and keep the length to one or two pages. Interview preparation involves researching the company, arriving early, dressing professionally, and having questions ready for the interviewer. Candidates must be prepared for technical questions about their skills and projects, using a "what, why, how, and troubleshooting" framework to explain concepts.
