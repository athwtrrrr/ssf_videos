# VIDEO: A Small Contribution A Big Impact (2025) | CHANNEL: Shaping STEMS Futures  | DATE: 2025-12-15

Coral reefs among the most biologically diverse and ecologically important ecosystems on Earth. But they are increasingly threatened by coral bleaching triggered when corals expel their symbiotic algae zusenthely due to heat stress and ocean acidification. Coral probiotic have been shown to induce coral resistant to stress and diseases which can help mitigate bleaching event in the coral system.
