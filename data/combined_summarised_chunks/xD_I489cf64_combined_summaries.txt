# VIDEO: Celestina Jullian Mae (2021) | CHANNEL: Shaping STEMS Futures  | DATE: 2025-08-18

Malaysia has a dependency on fossil fuels for the production of electricity with the values being at 90% in 2015. Most hydroelectric power plants in Malaysia are still utilizing coal in electricity production. For a country that is strategically located between the oceans, it will be wise for them to utilize ocean thermal energy conversion, also known as OTCH.
